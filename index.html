Welcome to EGCSS, a Kuwait-based registered company specializing in Shipping, International Freight Forwarding, and Logistics. We offer comprehensive Sea, Air, and Overland transportation services to meet your business needs.

Founded by an experienced shipping professional with over 20 years of industry expertise, we have successfully navigated all facets of the logistics sector, from working with ship owners to providing Total Logistics solutions.

Our services encompass Free Zone activities, Air and Sea freight forwarding, NVOCC agency representation, Ship Chandelling, and Project Handling. At EGCSS, we value your time and are always ready to support your logistics needs.

With EGCSS, you'll be prepared for new movements anytime. Why Choose Us? Simply put, we care about your business and your success.

Our Expertise & Experience

EGCSS was established in 2007 by a dedicated team of professionals committed to providing exceptional logistics services. Our company was built on the foundation of delivering the highest quality service at the most competitive rates, ensuring our clients' ability to thrive in a challenging market environment.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction has earned us long-standing relationships with our clients, many of whom have continued with us for years. We take pride in our ability to adapt to the dynamic logistics landscape while maintaining our focus on delivering value-driven solutions.

Our Services

Sea Freight

EGCSS offers regular shipping services, including:

  • - Full Container Load (FCL)
  • - Less than Container Load (LCL)
  • - Special equipment shipping

Our sea freight services include:

  • - Regular freight consolidation.
  • - Vessel chartering (Voyage or Time).
  • - Door-to-door delivery.
  • - Project cargo handling.
  • - Advisory services for fresh and hazardous cargo.
  • - Bulk transportation using Flexi Tanks and ISO Tanks.

Trucking

EGCSS provides reliable trucking services across the GCC, North Africa, and the Middle East, including:

  • - Low-bed trailers for oversized shipments.
  • - Reefer trailers for fresh and frozen products.

Storage

Our storage facility in the Suez Canal Economic Zone, near Sokhna Port, covers 25,000 square meters and supports seamless logistics.

Air Freight

EGCSS secures competitive air freight rates through partnerships with airlines, handling perishable goods, hazardous cargo, and time-sensitive products.

  • - Global air freight consolidation.
  • - Aircraft chartering worldwide.
