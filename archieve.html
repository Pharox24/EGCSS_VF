About EGCSS

EGCSS is a Kuwait-based, registered company specializing in shipping, international freight forwarding, and logistics. We provide comprehensive transportation solutions across sea, air, and overland routes, ensuring efficient and reliable movement of goods globally. Our extensive network and industry expertise allow us to cater to a wide range of clients, from individuals to large corporations, helping them achieve seamless supply chain operations.
Our services are tailored to meet the evolving demands of modern businesses, offering end-to-end logistics support that includes customs clearance, warehousing, and distribution. Whether it’s managing complex logistics chains or handling urgent shipments, EGCSS is committed to delivering excellence with precision and speed.

Our Expertise & Experience

Founded by a seasoned shipping professional with over 20 years of hands-on industry experience, EGCSS brings deep-rooted knowledge and a thorough understanding of global logistics. Our founder's journey spans various aspects of the shipping industry—beginning with direct work alongside ship owners, progressing into total logistics solutions, and covering every facet of the supply chain.

Our capabilities extend beyond standard freight forwarding services to include:

    Overall Free Zone Activities: Expertise in managing warehousing, distribution, and value-added services within free zones.
    Freight Forwarding (Air & Sea): Tailored solutions to move cargo efficiently via major trade routes worldwide.
    NVOCC (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier): Acting as an intermediary to offer flexible shipping solutions.
    Ship Chandelling: Providing comprehensive supplies and support services to vessels at ports.
    Project Handling: Managing specialized logistics for oversized, complex, and time-sensitive projects.

With a solid foundation and a commitment to innovation, EGCSS has built a reputation for reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
